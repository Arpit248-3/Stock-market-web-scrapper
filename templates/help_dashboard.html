How to Use StockScraper

1. Analytics Dashboard

This is your main hub for market analysis.

  • Select Stock: Use the dropdown for pre-selected top stocks.
  • Search Ticker: Enter any official NSE ticker symbol (e.g., RELIANCE.NS, TCS.NS) for custom analysis.
  • Time Period: Choose your desired time frame, from 5 days to 1 year.
  • Chart Type: Switch between Line, Area, and Candlestick charts.
  • Analyse: Click the button to fetch and display all data.

2. Trading Panel

Practice your trading strategy with a ₹1,00,000 mock portfolio.

  • Select Action: Choose "Buy" or "Sell".
  • Ticker: Enter the NSE ticker symbol you wish to trade.
  • Quantity: Enter the number of shares.
  • Execute: Your trade will be processed at the last market price.
  • Portfolio: Your holdings and cash balance will update automatically.

3. Data Scraper

Use this tool to fetch the latest financial news for any stock ticker.
